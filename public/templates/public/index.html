About Our School

Designed With Kids in Mind

Lofty Creek Christian School is an integrated Edu-care center catering to children from all walks of life. Located along Wambaa road, just 0.5km off the Nairobi-Nakuru Highway at Muthiga's Munyua road, we offer a safe, welcoming environment.

  • Kindergarten: Playgroup, PP1 & PP2
  • Primary: Grade 1 through Grade 6
  • Competency-Based & Montessori Curricula
  • Strong Christian Foundation
