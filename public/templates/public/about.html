Our Story

Nurturing Heritage From God

Lofty Creek Christian School is an integrated Edu-care center for children from all walks of life. We cater to children starting with the Kindergarten through Primary School Grade 6.

Located along Wambaa road, 0.5km off the Nairobi-Nakuru Highway at Muthiga's Munyua road, we have established ourselves as a beacon of quality Christian education in the region.

Designed with kids in mind, LCCS observes very high standards of learning with a holistic child development approach. Given the varied capabilities of our children, we have integrated Competency-Based and Montessori Curricula, all with a Christian worldview.

"At LCCS, your child will find a setting that is safe, welcoming and competent to enable them become the best they can be."

Our Mission

To transform this generation through sound education with a distinctly Christian worldview. We take our responsibility as educators seriously and with deep reverence, nurturing children who will become an influence for good in the world.
