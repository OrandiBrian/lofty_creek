Our Approach

Integrating CBC with Christian Values

At Lofty Creek Christian School, our academic programs are carefully structured to align with the Kenyan Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) while integrating robust Christian teachings. We focus on developing the whole child – mentally, physically, spiritually, and socially.

Critical Thinking

Encouraging students to ask questions, solve problems, and think creatively.

Practical Skills

Hands-on learning through activities, projects, and real-world applications.

Learning Levels

Comprehensive Educational Journey

Early Years (ECDE)

Playgroup, PP1, and PP2

Our kindergarten program heavily utilizes the Montessori method, emphasizing self-directed activity, hands-on learning, and collaborative play. Children are guided to make creative choices while discovering the world.

  • Sensory learning & Motor skills
  • Basic literacy and numeracy
  • Social skills and values

Primary School

Grade 1 through Grade 6

Implementing the full Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), our primary school prepares students to be engaged, empowered, and ethical citizens. We focus on continuous assessment over summative grading.

  • Core CBC subjects & continuous assessment
  • Digital literacy & practical arts
  • Christian Religious Education

Core Subjects & Competencies

Languages (Eng & Kiswahili) Mathematics Environmental Science CRE Digital Literacy Creative Arts Physical Education
