How to Apply

Admissions Process

Follow these simple steps to enroll your child at Lofty Creek Christian School.

1

Inquiry & Tour

Contact us or visit our school to learn more about our curriculum and environment.

2

Application

Obtain, fill out, and submit the application form along with required documents.

3

Assessment

An interactive session or formal assessment to understand your child's needs.

4

Enrollment

Receive acceptance letter, pay admission fees, and officially join our family.

What You Need

Admission Requirements

Please ensure you have the following documents ready when submitting your application:

  • Birth Certificate

    A clear copy of the child's birth certificate

  • Passport Photos

    2 recent passport size photographs of the child

  • Previous Reports

    Academic reports from previous school (if applicable)

  • Medical Records

    Immunization records and any relevant medical information

Our Programmes

Levels & Grades

Kindergarten (ECDE)

We offer a blend of Montessori and Competency-Based Curriculum approaches for our youngest learners. A strong foundation combining play, creativity, and foundational literacy/numeracy.

Lower Primary (Grades 1-3)

Fostering independence and advancing literacy and numeracy through engaging projects. Fully compliant with the CBC guidelines integrating Christian values.

Upper Primary (Grades 4-6)

Preparing students for Junior Secondary transitions. Focus is on depth of knowledge, critical thinking, practical skills, and character building.
